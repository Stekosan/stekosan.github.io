About

Data Science is a new passion and Environmental challenges is like my Ikigai or purpose.

I remembered when I was diving into the ocean in the Sulawasi Indonesia, Bora Bora in Polynesia and Palau Islands, the beauty of the underwater world left me in awe.

“Decoding nature’s distress signals: Using advanced analytics to protect our oceans, forests, and ecosystem”

However, it also opened my eyes to the growing threats our oceans face.

This realization, combined with my growing interest in environmental issues and the potential of AI, led me to explore how Data Science can be leveraged to address these critical environmental challenges.

In the realm of ocean conservation, I am earger to learn how to use image recognition AI to detect and quantify micro-plastics in ocean, streamlining the process of monitoring ocean health.

Additionally, I’m exploring how big data analytics engineering can help track the sources and movement of plastic pollution, informing targeted cleanup efforts and policy decisions for environmental agencies.

My goal is to bridge the gap between cutting-edge Data Science techniques and pressing environmental issues. By harnessing the power of AI and data-driven insights, I believe we can develop more effective solutions to combat air pollution, reduce ocean plastic, and ultimately create a more sustainable future for our planet.

Through my portfolio, I hope to showcase how Data Science can be a powerful tool for environmental stewardship, inspiring others to join in using technology for the greater good.
